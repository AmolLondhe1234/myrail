Indian Railways FAQ

1. How can I book a train ticket?

You can book a train ticket through the official Indian Railways website, IRCTC, or through authorized booking counters at railway stations. Tickets can also be booked via various mobile apps and travel agents.

2. What are the different classes of travel available?

Indian Railways offers various classes such as First Class AC (1A), Second Class AC (2A), Third Class AC (3A), Sleeper Class (SL), and General Class. Each class provides different levels of comfort and amenities.

3. How can I check the PNR status of my ticket?

You can check your PNR status on the IRCTC website, through SMS, or using various mobile apps. Simply enter your 10-digit PNR number to see the status of your booking.

4. Can I cancel my train ticket? What is the refund policy?

Yes, you can cancel your train ticket online through the IRCTC website or at any railway booking counter. The refund amount depends on the time of cancellation and the class of ticket. For detailed information, visit the IRCTC refund policy page.

5. What facilities are available for senior citizens?

Senior citizens are eligible for concessions on train tickets. Additionally, there are special quota seats reserved for them. You can request assistance for boarding and alighting at railway stations.

6. How can I find the schedule of a specific train?

You can find the schedule of any train on the Indian Railways website or through mobile apps by entering the train number or name. The schedule includes arrival and departure times at various stations.
